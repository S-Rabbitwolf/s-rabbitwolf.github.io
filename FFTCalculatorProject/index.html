                Each time a character levels up, their stats increase based on the job they are currently in.
                Internally, stats are stored as large values. On level up, growth is calculated as:
            


            

                Growth = floor(Raw Stat ÷ (Growth Constant + Level))
            


            

                Lower growth constants result in better stat growth. As level increases, growth slows down.
                Stats only increase when they reach the next whole number. This function is applied in reverse when leveling down.
            


            

                After growth is applied, internal values are converted into displayed stats using:
            


            

                Displayed Stat = floor(Raw Stat × Job Multiplier ÷ 1,638,400)
            


            

                The job used while leveling affects growth, and the final job affects displayed stats.
                Different job paths will produce different results.
